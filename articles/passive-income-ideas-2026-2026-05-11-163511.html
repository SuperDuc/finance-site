Passive Income in 2026: What's Working Right Now

Investing 2026-05-11 · 5 min read
← All Guides

Dividend Investing

Buy shares of dividend-paying stocks or ETFs and receive quarterly payments. A 4% dividend yield on $10,000 = $400/year — $33/month — for doing nothing after the initial purchase.

Best dividend ETFs: SCHD (Schwab US Dividend Equity), VYM (Vanguard High Dividend Yield), JEPI (JPMorgan Equity Premium Income, ~7% yield).

Use Robinhood or Fidelity to set up automatic reinvestment (DRIP) and watch compounding accelerate.

Crypto Staking

Staking means locking up cryptocurrency to help validate blockchain transactions. In return, you earn rewards — essentially interest on your crypto.

Current approximate staking yields:
• Ethereum (ETH): ~3–4% annually
• Solana (SOL): ~6–7% annually

Gemini offers staking on multiple assets directly from the platform — no technical setup required.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate income is passive once set up. Write a review once. Share a link once. Earn commissions for months as people find the content organically.

Best niches by commission: finance (highest per conversion), software/SaaS (recurring monthly commissions), health (massive audience).
